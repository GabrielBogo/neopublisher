WHAT DATA DO WE COLLECT?

NEOPUB collects the following data:

  • Browsing behavior data (links, clicks, scrolls, time spent on page, geolocation, browser and device information, website address you came from before landing on our web page)

HOW DO WE COLLECT YOUR DATA?

You directly provide NEOPUB with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies, if you provide us with your consent.

HOW WILL WE USE YOUR DATA??

NEOPUB collects your data so that we can:

  • Understand you as a customer to be able to improve our business through better offers, more relevant content and better spent advertisement.

HOW DO WE STORE YOUR DATA?

NEOPUB securely may store your data at Google Big Query Data Warehouse as well as at third-party vendors such as Google Analytics, both of which hold extremely high reputation on applying best practices to keep your privacy secure.

NEOPUB will keep your data while it’s useful for the purposes listed above. When it’s no longer needed by our internal operations, we will delete your data from all our databases within one year.

IS THERE INTERNATIONAL TRANSFER OF YOUR DATA?

Your data might be transferred or stored in international networks and servers but always within the EU or to a country outside the EU that is considered “adequate” by the European Commission.

WHO DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL DATA WITH?

We will never sell your personal data to anyone, and we may share it only with Data Processors or Data Storage providers like the ones mentioned on the item above.

WHAT ARE YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS?

The right to access - You have the right to request NEOPUB for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification - You have the right to request that NEOPUB correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request NEOPUB to complete information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure — You have the right to request that NEOPUB erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that NEOPUB restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to NEOPUB’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability - You have the right to request that NEOPUB transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: contact@neopublisher.com

WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

HOW DO WE USE COOKIES?

NEOPUB uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in, when applicable
  • Understanding how you use our website

WHAT TYPES OF COOKIES DO WE USE?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality — NEOPUB uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.
  • Advertising — NEOPUB uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. NEOPUB sometimes shares some limited aspects of this data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may also share online data collected through cookies with our advertising partners. This means that when you visit another website, you may be shown advertising based on your browsing patterns on our website.
  • Analytics — NEOPUB uses these cookies to understand your behaviour with our website, apps and emails, so we can learn your interests as a customer as well as ways to serve you better.

HOW TO MANAGE COOKIES

When you first land on our website, you will be prompted to provide your consent with respect to the types of cookies we are allowed to place on your browser. Cookies of type “Functionality” will not depend on your consent since they are required for the website to function properly. You may change or revoke your consent at any time by clicking at the MANAGE CONSENT option in the website footer.

You can set your browser not to accept any cookies as well as remove all cookies from your browser. Visit https://www.allaboutcookies.org/manage-cookies/ for more information on how to perform those actions. Bear in mind, however, that by doing that some of our website features may not function as a result.

PRIVACY POLICIES OF OTHER WEBSITES

The NEOPUB website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY

NEOPUB keeps its Privacy Policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. When we do that, we will revise the “Last Updated” date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

If there are any material changes to this Privacy Policy we may notify you, provided that we have your contact information in our databases.

We encourage you to periodically review this Privacy Policy to learn how NEOPUB is processing your data.

HOW TO CONTACT US

If you have any questions about NEOPUB’s Privacy Policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us at contact@neopublisher.com.
